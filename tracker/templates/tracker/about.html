About SkillHub

SkillHub is a platform designed to help you manage, track, and achieve your personal learning goals. Whether you're learning to code, picking up a new language, or developing a creative hobby â€” SkillHub gives you the tools to stay on track.

Key Features

  • Set and manage custom learning goals
  • Track progress over time
  • Organize skills by category and difficulty
  • Record useful resources like videos, articles, and books
  • Collaborate with others by sharing public skill updates (future feature!)

Who is it for?

SkillHub is for students, professionals, and lifelong learners who want to take control of their self-improvement journey. It's built with simplicity, structure, and encouragement in mind.
